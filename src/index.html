About Us

Solo Cafe is located in a cozy corner in the heart of Santa Catalina, Palma de Mallorca. In our space, we are passionate about offering you a unique experience with every visit, combining the best of a relaxed atmosphere with a delicious, healthy, and flavorful menu.

We specialize in brunch and lunch, with fresh and creative options that cater to all tastes. From energy-packed dishes to start your day to lunches that will make you feel right at home, always with quality ingredients and local products. Additionally, we are veggie and vegan-friendly, so you'll find a wide variety of options that respect your lifestyle.

Our coffee is our pride. We select only the freshest beans and prepare them with care so you can enjoy the perfect cup, accompanied by natural smoothies full of flavor and freshness.

We invite you to visit us and enjoy a place where great food and the best company come together. Whether for a relaxed brunch with friends, a coffee to start your morning right, or a healthy lunch, we are waiting for you with open arms!
